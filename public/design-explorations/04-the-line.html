Quang Duong,
and the quiet
Vietnamese ascent.

Nineteen months ago, Quang Duong was playing exhibition doubles at a shopping mall in District 7. This weekend he will walk onto a PPA Tour court in Austin seeded #12 — the first Vietnamese player inside the world's top 20.

His story reflects something larger: a generation of Southeast Asian players who skipped tennis entirely, went straight to pickleball, and are now quietly rewriting what the international field looks like.
